About Big Three Performance

Meet Logan – The Founder

Logan has been passionate about performance cars since a young age. With years of experience in the automotive industry, he turned his love for cars into Big Three Performance. What started as a personal hobby quickly evolved into a full-service performance shop dedicated to bringing out the best in every vehicle.

Our Mission

At Big Three Performance, our goal is to deliver top-quality automotive services, whether you're looking for standard maintenance or high-performance upgrades. We believe in precision, expertise, and pushing the limits of automotive performance.
